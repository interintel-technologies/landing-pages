About Us

What Is NenaSasa

NenaSasa is a unified customer engagement and communications platform designed for businesses that operate across voice and messaging channels.

Instead of managing disconnected tools for calls, chat, automation, and messaging, NenaSasa provides a single platform to run customer interactions end to end — from automated self-service to human-assisted support and system-level integrations.

Learn more
Our Mission

Why NenaSasa

Customer communication today is fragmented across channels, teams, and tools. This leads to operational inefficiencies, inconsistent customer experiences, and limited visibility.

NenaSasa eliminates this fragmentation by unifying execution, automation, oversight, and integration into one modular platform — allowing businesses to scale customer engagement without increasing complexity.

How It All Works Together

NenaSasa is modular by design, but unified in operation.

Use NenaCall for frontline agent execution.

Manage teams and performance with NenaVoice.

Engage customers through live and AI-assisted chat using NenaChat.

Automate high-volume, repetitive interactions with NenaBot.

Reach customers instantly with NenaSMS.

Embed all communication capabilities into products and workflows via NenaSDK.

All interactions operate within one platform architecture, enabling consistent customer experiences and centralized operational visibility.
